Features

Help is available to you anytime

Curated Content

We understand that finding the right help and resources for your mental health can be an overwhelming process. It is our primary goal to get you the help you need with the most relevant resources tailored to your particular mental health needs.

Chat with Professionals

We have partnered with most caring, most experienced healthcare professional in the field of mental wellbeing. When life does not give you enough time to schedule in-person appointments, use our expert chat feature to connect to one of our partnered therapist to help you on your healing journey.

Track your Progress

Keeping track of your mental health is crucial in the healing process. User the "My Progress" feature on our app to keep a personal journal to allow for better introspection and improvement of symptoms.

Our App

Begin your healing journey

  • Create a profile

    Just answer a few questions about your mental health

    Silverlining is designed to help you take charge of your mental health! Take the time to really get to know yourself with our therapist-approved questionnaire.

  • Track your healing journey

    The Mood Journal is quick and easy to use

    It can be so easy to lose track of all the emotions we experience day in, day out. With Silverlining you don’t have to worry about forgetting where you’ve been. Your emotions just got their own personalized journal.

  • Find a therapist

    Chat with a therapist

    Silverlining features therapists from all different specialties, from treating clinical depression to trauma recovery. The versatile chat function lets you quickly and easily set up video calls, audio calls, or just simply chat via text.

  • Improve your mental health with Silverlining

    Be consistent for you. You owe it to yourself

    The best way to get the most out of your life is to keep your mental health strong just like your physical health. Silverlining gives you the tools you need to stay mentally healthy and care for your emotional well-being. It’s time to see the silver lining to life’s experiences.
